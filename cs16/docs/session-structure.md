- 110 min: Lab build
- 20 min: Reading/annotation
- 30 min: Debugging/instrumentation
- 20 min: Reflection (log + next steps)

1-hour sessions (Tue/Wed/Fri)
- 10 min: Retrieval
- 30 min: Drills/exercises
- 20 min: Feynman write-up or diagram
